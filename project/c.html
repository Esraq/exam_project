Personal Summary: Mr. kabir Bin Anwar is a career civil servant. He belongs to Bangladesh civil service (administration) cadre. He comes of a noble Muslim family. He was born in 1964 on 1st January at Sirajgonj. Mr.Kabir’s father Mr. Anwar Hossain is a freedom fighter and a businessman by profession who is actively involved in many socio-organizational activities. Mr. Kabir’s mother Sayada Isabela was an active organizer of the historic event of the War of Liberation. Moreover, she was a teacher and litterateur who has authored 22 books. Mr. Kabir’s Maternal grandfather Syed Ishaq Hossain Siraji was a lawyer who was the brother, teacher & musician of the renowned Bengali writer & poet - Syed Ismail Hossain Siraji. Mr. Kabir’s spouse name is Mrs.Toufika Kabir. He is blessed by one son and two daughters.

Education: Mr. kabir Bin Anwar completed his schooling and intermediate level from Residential Model School & College, Dhaka. Thereafter he got admitted at the University of Dhaka in the department of Economics. Mr.Kabir has completed MSS in Political Science. He has also obtained L.L.B degree.


Professional Experiance:Mr. Kabir joined civil service on 15 February in 1988 as Assistant commissioner. In his long colorful and successful service career he shouldered many important responsibilities in the field administration as well as in the central tire of Bureaucracy. The important assignments he shouldered as Assistant Commissioner/Assistant Commissioner (Land)/Upazila Nirbahi Officer. He has also served Ministry of Foreign affairs as Assistant Secretary, Embassy of Bangladesh, The Hague, Netherlands as First Secretary/ Finance Division as Senior Assistant Secretary/ Ministry of Home Affairs as Deputy Secretary. Prior to the current appointment he served the Prime Minister’s Office as the Director (Administration). Presently, Mr. Kabir (Additional Secretary), is working as the Director General (Admin), Prime Minister’s Office. In addition to that Mr Kabir is also performing outstanding role as the Project Director of the Acess to Information (A2i-2) Project/ Head of Implementation committee of the Asharayan -2 Project ( a project designed by Prime Minister’s Office to ensure residence of the Homeless poor People)/ Head of the inter Ministerial Committee to provide Develop Assistnace to the Minor Ethnic Community (Except Chitttagong Hill Tracts).

Skills:Mr. Anwar is pioneer of new ideas and skill. He is deeply associated with the concept of Digital Bangladesh. He has Experience in international negotiation, Expertise on biodiversity conservation, Access and Benefit sharing. Mr.Kabir is accustomed with knowledge of e-governance. His mother tongue is Bangla. He has proficiency in English. Mr. Kabir is reliable, highly resourceful, flexible, innovative and enthusiastic individual who possesses a considerable amount of knowledge regarding administrative and office procedures. He is quick learner who can absorb new ideas and is experienced in coordinating, planning and organizing a wide range of administrative activities.
