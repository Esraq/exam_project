About the foundation


1.The name of our foundation is Isabela foundation
2.a)REGISTERED OFFICE:The registered office of the Foundation shall be situated at kalibari Road,Sirajgong Sadar,Sirajganj District,Bangladesh
b)AREA OF OPERATION:ALL over Bangladesh.
c)The Society will be abide all rules,regulations and laws of the country.
The Society will be held responsible for their own activities.
Registering authority will not be responsible for activity of the registered entity.

3.Nature of the Foundation


It is Non-political,Non-profitable,Non-government & Charitable Foundation.

4.OBJECTIVES OF THE FOUNDATION:

Subject to obtaining necessary permissions from the government/ concerned authority/ coperation/ competent authority,the objects for which the Foundation is established are all or any of the following,so long as they do not contradict the provision of Section 20 of the Act in which case they shall be treated as ineffective:


1. To work for nature conservation and sustainable development of the country.
2. To work on policy research,strategy and planning on nature conservation,biodiversity,wildlife,protected areas management,climate change adaption,resilience and susutainable development.
3. To promote and be actively involved in conservation of nature and natural resources and sustainable development of Bangladesh.
4. To organize charity programmes for vulnerable communities,women,children and orphans for their livelihood security and providing facilities like vocational training,non-formal school,orphanage,old homes,hospitals and generate employment opportunities.
5.To serve the destitute people,organize and establish rescue and rehebilitation centers.
6.To join and actively participate disaster (flood, cyclone, earthquake, epidemic diseases, accidents) prone victims rescue, relief and rehabilitation programmes of government and UN agencies.
7.To facilitate training in disaster response programme for the youths.
8.To organize and implement innovative livelihoods development programmes through poverty eradication, SME and employment generation to the youth and women.
9.To implement national policy and agenda on health care, livelihood security, education and sports.
10.To support and implement the cultural programme targeting national heritage.
11.To work on natural and cultural heritage and on the archeological research and conservation.
12.To mark the international days on environment, biodiversity, wildlife, wetlands, health, education and culture.
13.To implement programmes for the children towards healthy sports and culture.
14.To promote Science and Technology, ICT innovations and facilitate effective use of science engineering, ICT and technology to meet basic needs of symmetrical growth and sustainable development.
15.To conduct and expedite research on green technology to harness national resources, sustain the basic life-support systems of ecosystem and its values and functions.
16.En route to Foster Asian Regional Biodiversity Conservation and Sustain Rational Ecological Footprint.
17.Facilitate putting into practice the UN-Rio Conventions, Convention on Biological Diversity , Bonn Convention, CITES, UNFCCC, MARPOL and Ramsar Convention.
18.Develop ecological model and environmental management system for the threatened ecosystems.
19.Catalyze UN Conventions on Nature and Environment and promote implementation SDGs at national level for better environment and improved livelihoods.
20.Facilitate implementation of Government policy, strategy and programmes in achieving the agenda of NBSAP/BCCSAP and other policies pertaining to environment protection and sustainable development.
21.To conduct study and research on Biological Diversity for defining and designing the sustainable use plan of biological resources and ecological balance.
22.To conduct survey and inventoried the wild flora, fauna and ecosystems with focus on forest, wetlands, mangroves, floodplains, hills, costal and marine ecosystems.
23.To prepare digitized d-base of biological diversity of the country.
24.To establish ex situ gene bank and conduct research on wild flora and fauna.
25.To promote enhanced management actions of threatened wild flora, fauna and facilitate upscaling management planning for the Protected Areas (PA) and threatened ecosystems.
26.To conduct study and research on River Biota for updating and mainstreaming management regime of water and aquatic resources.
27.To conduct survey and research on rivers and wetlands, prepare National River Directory and d-base.
28.To establish ex-situ conservation area of the wild genome of rare plants and animal.
29.To develop biological bi-products and ecological agro-fisheries for green growth of the country through developing entrepreneurial skills of women and youth.
30.To establish wildlife and biodiversity farm/park/private reserve and train the poor and destitute young boys and girls for employment generation in green business sector.
31.To conduct research on captive breeding , propagation, re-introduction, rehabilitation of threatened wild flora and fauna.
32.To develop and implement ecological restoration of degraded habitats, forests, denuded hills and other ecosystems.
33.To facilitate identification of areas of special conservation significance and catalyze establishing and managing Community Conservation Area (CCA) through govt. defined co-management protocol.
34.To develop and implement programs on Climate Change adaptation, mitigation, resilience, REDD + and prevention.
35.To organize, nature camp, nature watch, nature fair eco and nature tourism.
36.To conduct and promote education program and research activities for generating as well as improving the knowledge and understanding of the indigenous flora, fauna, microorganism and vegetation of Bangladesh along with the terrestrial, marine and fresh water ecosystems.
37.To act on education communication and awareness programme portraying natural heritage, natural history, biological diversity, biological resources and Human-Environment interface of Bangladesh.
38.Comprehensive documentary on the status, resources, culture, archaeological heritage hydrology, erosion, accretion, sedimentation, floods, invertebrates, amphibians, fish, turtle, waterbirds, Gharial dolphin, mammals, and Charland diversity of the major river systems of Bangladesh.
39.To facilitate natural harmony of human being with nature through production of popular and lively documentary on nature, biological diversity and peopleâ€™s interaction with the same.
40.TV documentary production and telecast for general mass on nature, natural resources and biodiversity of Bangladesh for conservation and wise use of natural resources awareness building.
41.To conduct periodical review of the status of threatened wild floral and faunal species.
42.To monitor the environmental consequences of development project and organize green watch to correct the course.
43.To conduct research on Biotechnology, plant and animal genetics.
44.To conduct, promote, support and assist activities for preparation of Red Data Book on flora as well as fauna in Bangladesh.
45.To promote, support and implement programs for rehabilitation of terrestrial, marine and fresh water ecosystems in Bangladesh.
46.To promote, get involved and support activities for preventing and controlling Alien Invasive Species (AIS).
47.To conduct, promote and support biological studies on selected important communities/species with emphasis on keystone, flagship and bio indicator species.
48.To initiate programs and conduct capacity building as well as human resource development activities on nature and biodiversity conservation.
49.T conduct, promote and support GIS and remote sensing studies on floral and faunal diversity as well on selected threatened species.
50.To work for development of status report on nature conservation and biodiversity at regular intervals.
51.To work for development of status report on conservation of all listed threatened and protected species along with the listed ecosystems.
52.To work for preparation of status report on listed invasive species.
53.To monitor and report on any Genetically Modified Organism (GMO) that will be released to the environment.
54.To promote, support and assist to protect, maintain and conserve the threatened or selected floral and faunal species for ex-situ as well as in-situ conservation.
55.To collect, process, generate and disseminate information on biodiversity and sustainable use of indigenous bio-resources.
56.To conduct IEE, ESIA, EIA studies on the development projects.
57.To act as an advisory and consultative body for floral as well as faunal identification and provide advisory and information services on biodiversity and its conservation.
58.To conduct environment impact studies on floral, faunal and microbial diversity along with its terrestrial, marine and fresh water ecosystems.
59.To promote research fellowship on nature and biodiversity conservation.
60.To collaborate research programs on biodiversity with other organizations.
