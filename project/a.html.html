Along with her husband, she became involved in politics and cultural activities. In 1971, he participated in the liberation war and served as a nurse in the Rumi Liberation War camp. After the war, he started to study again. As well as work of social services. He had arranged for rehabilitation of 35 Biranganga rescued from various camps of Pak army.
