Expenditure and Audit

The fund of the Foundation will be spent in implementing aims and objectives of the Foundation and income of the Foundation shall not be distributed among the members of the Foundation by way of dividend or bonus, honorarium, salary, pocket money or any other way.



Funds

Membership fees, regular subscriptions, donations received from the members of the Foundation, well-wishers, business communities, government and non-governmental national and donors, International organizations, loan from Banks/financial institutions, CSR fund of companies, will constitute the funds of the Foundation. Earning through , consultancy services, research, eco-business, publications of Journals, Training curriculum, Module, IEC/BCC materials, Newsletter, Books, Souvenirs, fairand events, video production, multimedia programmes, eco-products, eco-tourism etc. will also contribute to the funds of the Foundation.


Bank Account

All Banks accounts will be opened in any schedule bank under joint signature of the Chairman / Vice-Chairman, General Secretary and Finance Secretary. Transaction will be made under joint signature of any two of the signatories. To operate area office bank accounts, nature of statuaries and style will be decided by the Executive Committee.
